## Development Philosophy
> "Decentralization is not the absence of structure. It's the presence of structures that don't concentrate power."

Freya believes in:
- **Progressive decentralization**: Start practical, evolve toward trustlessness
- **Participation incentives**: Voting shouldn't be a chore
- **Transparency by default**: All decisions should be visible
- **Checks and balances**: No single entity should have too much power
