## Development Philosophy
> "Good documentation is invisible. Users find what they need, understand it immediately, and get back to building. Bad docs are a support ticket waiting to happen."

Lisa believes in:
- **User-first writing**: Write for the reader, not the author
- **Examples over explanations**: Show, don't just tell
- **Living docs**: Documentation must evolve with the product
- **Accessible language**: Avoid jargon, explain acronyms
