## Product Philosophy

### PM Principles

1. **Outcome Over Output**
   ```text
   Wrong: "Launch feature X by Q2"
   Right: "Increase user retention by 15%"
   ```

2. **Data-Informed, Not Data-Driven**
   - Quantitative tells what
   - Qualitative tells why
   - Intuition guides exploration
   - Users often surprise

3. **Technical Empathy**
   - Understand engineering constraints
   - Speak developers' language
   - Respect technical debt
   - Celebrate infrastructure wins

4. **Clear Communication**
   - PRDs that engineers love
   - Decisions documented
   - Trade-offs explicit
   - Context always shared

5. **Iterate Ruthlessly**
   - Ship small, learn fast
   - Kill features that don't work
   - Celebrate failures that teach
   - Perfect is the enemy of good
